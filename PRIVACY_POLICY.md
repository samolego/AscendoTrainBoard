# Privacy Policy for AscendoTrainBoard

**Last Updated:** January 2, 2025

## Introduction

AscendoTrainBoard ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our climbing training application.

**Important:** You can use most features of AscendoTrainBoard without creating an account. Browsing sectors, viewing climbing problems, and seeing ratings can all be done anonymously without providing any personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you register for an account, we collect:
- **Username:** A unique identifier you choose for your account
- **Password:** Stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- **Account Creation Date:** Timestamp of when your account was created

### User-Generated Content
When you use the app, you may create:
- **Climbing Problems:** Route information including name, description, grade, and hold sequences
- **Ratings and Reviews:** Your grade suggestions and star ratings (1-5) for climbing problems
- **Problem Modifications:** Updates to climbing problems you've created

### Technical Information
- **Authentication Tokens:** Session tokens to keep you logged in
- **IP Address:** Temporarily used for rate limiting and security purposes to prevent brute force attacks

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:
- Provide and maintain your account functionality
- Allow you to create, edit, and delete climbing problems
- Display your username as the author of problems you create
- Calculate average grades and ratings for climbing problems
- Prevent unauthorized access and abuse through rate limiting
- Provide administrative functions (for admin users only)

## Data Storage and Security

- All passwords are hashed and encrypted before storage
- Authentication uses secure bearer token system
- We implement rate limiting to protect against brute force attacks
- Failed login attempts result in temporary IP-based restrictions

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.** 

The only information visible to other users is:
- Your username (displayed as the author of climbing problems you create)
- Climbing problems you create (name, description, grade, hold sequences)
- Your ratings and grade suggestions on climbing problems

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all climbing problems and ratings you've created
- **Modify:** Edit or update your climbing problems at any time
- **Delete:** Remove climbing problems you've created
- **Account Deletion:** Contact an administrator to delete your account and associated data

## Children's Privacy

**Anonymous Use:** Children of any age can use AscendoTrainBoard to browse sectors and view climbing problems without creating an account, as no personal information is collected in this mode.

**Account Creation:** We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13 who creates an account. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 through account registration, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. Creating an account, submitting problems, or rating climbs requires users to be at least 13 years old.

## Data Retention

- Account information is retained as long as your account is active
- Climbing problems and ratings are retained until you delete them
- Deleted content is permanently removed from our systems
- Session tokens expire and are automatically invalidated upon logout

## Third-Party Services

AscendoTrainBoard does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use any tracking tools, cookies (beyond authentication), analytics services, or advertising technologies to monitor your behavior or collect usage statistics.

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on:
- **Contractual Necessity:** To provide the service you've requested
- **Legitimate Interests:** To maintain security and prevent abuse
- **Consent:** For account creation and use of the service

## Summary

In short, AscendoTrainBoard:
- ✅ Can be used anonymously without any data collection (browsing only)
- ✅ Only collects essential information if you choose to create an account
- ✅ Does not sell or share your data with third parties
- ✅ Does not use tracking, analytics, or advertising
- ✅ Allows you to control and delete your content
- ✅ Implements security measures to protect your data
- ✅ Is transparent about what data we collect and why

By using AscendoTrainBoard, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
